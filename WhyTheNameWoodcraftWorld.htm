The story behind the WoodcraftWorld name is a strange one.

When I was 8 (I know way too young), I created my YouTube channel in 2017 and chose the name "Steve Gamer". I don't know why, it's a terrible name

Then, at some point in 2018, i realised that this was a terrible name and changed it to "The WoodCraft World", a combination of Wood + Minecraft + World.

then in 2019, I consolidated to just "WoodcraftWorld" and have used it for literally everything else, from Reddit to VRChat, from PlayStation to Nintendo Network. When considering getting a domain name at TheUKDomain.uk (registrar for .co.uk) I found out that WoodcraftWorld.co.uk has been taken by a woodworking company founded and registered with Companies House (UK equivalent to the SEC) in 2022. I HAD THE NAME FIRST! I CAME UP WITH WOODCRAFTWORLD IN 2018 AND YOU CAME UP WITH "WOODCRAFT WORLD LIMITED" IN 2022
